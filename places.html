Ainavilli

Famous for the Sidhi Vinayaka Temple, Ainavilli attracts thousands of devotees every year. The village is also surrounded by lush coconut groves and rivers, giving it a serene atmosphere for tourists.

Antarvedi

A spiritual place where the Godavari River meets the Bay of Bengal. The Antarvedi Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple is a must-visit for pilgrims and tourists alike. Scenic backwaters make it perfect for photography.

Appanapalli

Popular during Sankranti and other local festivals, Appanapalli showcases traditional village life and temple culture. Visitors can enjoy local cuisine and the colorful festive celebrations.

Draksharamam

Home to one of the Pancharama Kshetras, the Bhimeswara Swamy Temple in Draksharamam is an architectural marvel. Pilgrims visit here for its spiritual significance and beautiful temple carvings.

Uppada Beach

A serene beach famous for its scenic beauty and Uppada Jamdani Sarees. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers, this location offers calm waters and breathtaking sunsets.
